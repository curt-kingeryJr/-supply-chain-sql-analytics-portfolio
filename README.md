Healthcare Supply Chain Analytics Portfolio

Overview

This repository demonstrates end-to-end SQL analytics projects focused on healthcare supply chain operations. The projects replicate real-world analytical workflows using synthetic data and anonymized business scenarios.

The objective of these projects is to transform operational data into decision-ready insights by applying data cleaning, feature engineering, aggregation, and business performance analysis techniques.

Projects

Project 1: Survey Participation Analytics

Business Problem

Leaders need visibility into participation trends, engagement levels, and operational feedback across geographically distributed organizations.

Analytical Objectives

* Clean and standardize survey data
* Create reusable analytical views
* Measure survey completion duration
* Identify repeat participants
* Analyze participation by role and region
* Detect outliers and unusual patterns
* Produce executive-ready metrics and insights

Key Skills Demonstrated

* Data cleaning
* Feature engineering
* Descriptive statistics
* Window functions
* Outlier analysis
* KPI development
* Executive reporting

Project 2: Open Order & Backorder Risk Monitoring

Business Problem

Supply organizations need visibility into aging orders, disruption drivers, and high-impact inventory issues that may affect operational readiness.

Analytical Objectives

* Integrate multiple supplier datasets
* Standardize inconsistent reason categories
* Analyze order aging patterns
* Measure issue prevalence and concentration
* Identify high-cost and high-volume items
* Surface data quality concerns
* Support risk prioritization and decision-making

Key Skills Demonstrated

* Data modeling
* Data integration
* Business rule implementation
* CTE development
* Window functions
* Percent-of-total calculations
* Risk analytics
* Data quality assessment

Portfolio Philosophy

The projects in this repository emphasize business impact over technical complexity. The focus is on demonstrating the ability to convert messy operational data into clear, actionable recommendations that support decision-making.

All datasets are synthetic and have been anonymized for educational and portfolio purposes.
